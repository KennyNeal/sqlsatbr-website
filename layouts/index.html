About

Day of Data Baton Rouge is a free, community-driven event hosted by the Baton Rouge User Groups for IT and Microsoft Data Platform professionals, students, and tech enthusiasts. Whether your interests are Business Intelligence, Analytics, Azure SQL, or SQL Server, the event is built to help you grow your skills and connect with the local data community.

Attendees get a full day of expert-led technical sessions from SQL Server experts, Microsoft MVPs, and published authors, plus career-development insights, food, and networking — all at no cost. It's an approachable, all-levels event: whether you're just getting started or have years of experience, there's a track for you.

Our history

Day of Data Baton Rouge is now in its 17th year. The event began in 2009 as SQL Saturday Baton Rouge, part of the global SQLSaturday community series, and the Archive preserves public records back to that first event. Over the years the event has grown into today's Day of Data Baton Rouge, still hosted by the same Baton Rouge User Groups community. Browse the Archive for past Event Years, or see the broader SQLSaturday Baton Rouge history for the earlier legacy record.
