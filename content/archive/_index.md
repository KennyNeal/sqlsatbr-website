Day of Data Baton Rouge is now in its **17th year**. The event began in **2009** as **SQL Saturday Baton Rouge**, part of the global SQLSaturday community series, and has grown into today's Day of Data Baton Rouge — still hosted by the same Baton Rouge User Groups community. The entries below capture that history back to the first event, each linking to the legacy SQLSaturday archive for full session and speaker details.
